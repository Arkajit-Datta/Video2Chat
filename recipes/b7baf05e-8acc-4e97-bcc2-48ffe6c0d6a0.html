Garlic Bread with Fresh Clams and Oysters

This recipe brings together the succulent taste of fresh clams and oysters with the aromatic crunch of homemade garlic bread. Served with a side of lemon wedges and optional dipping sauces, this dish is perfect as an appetizer or a light main course.

Step 1

Preheat your oven to 375F (190C) for the garlic bread. Meanwhile, place clams and oysters in the freezer for 10 to 15 minutes to make them easier to shuck.

Step 2

For the garlic bread, mix together 1/2 cup of softened unsalted butter, 3 minced garlic cloves, 1 tablespoon of freshly chopped parsley, and a pinch of salt. Cut a baguette into 1/2-inch slices without cutting all the way through, and spread the garlic butter mixture between the slices.

Step 3

Wrap the garlic bread in aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 10 minutes. Then, uncover and bake for an additional 5 minutes or until the edges are golden and crispy.

Step 4

While the bread is baking, start shucking the clams and oysters using the techniques described in the provided recipes, ensuring that they are clean and ready to be served.

Step 5

Arrange the shucked clams and oysters on a serving platter with lemon wedges and optional sauces such as cocktail sauce, mignonette, or a simple vinaigrette.

Step 6

Once the garlic bread is done, let it cool slightly before serving alongside the fresh clams and oysters.

Step 7

Garnish the platter with additional chopped parsley or chives for color and serve immediately while the bread is warm and the seafood is chilled.
